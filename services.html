How We Deliver

Project Process

We follow a well-established method of delivery that breaks every project into three clear phases.

I
Phase One

Plans & Estimate

We receive the plans and provide a cost estimate. Upon agreement of terms, we begin laying the pipe work before the ground floor slab.

II
Phase Two

Foundation Work

The grinder meets the walls. We lay the foundation for all future work — the project takes shape and we establish exactly what is needed.

III
Phase Three

Finishings

All finishings are completed in close coordination with the construction team — a collaborative effort to deliver a project that exceeds expectations.

Payment Structure

For large projects, work begins after the first third is paid. The second third is due at the midpoint. The final third is payable at completion. Custom terms available on request.
