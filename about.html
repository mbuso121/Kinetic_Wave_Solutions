Our Story

Built by
entrepreneurs

Kinetic Wave Solutions is an evolution of Kluetics Electrical — an electrical installation company. We took the experience and knowledge built there and redirected it with one ultimate goal: being the best company in green technology.

The very nature of us being built by entrepreneurs has allowed the company to evolve beyond its origins. As responsible corporate citizens, we saw it fit to shift our focus to green technology — to make the world a better place and better compete in the marketplace.

How We Are Organised

Company
Structure

At Kinetic Wave Solutions we recognise that it takes more than knowing the actual work to run a company. We are a healthy mix of technical and non-technical people.

The technical team oversees the actual work while the non-technical team ensures we are a fully functional and compliant business. All management is actively involved.

We maintain a strong financial position by making investments only as we can afford — never relying on lines of credit.
